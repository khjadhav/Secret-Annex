Anne's Room

Anne’s room was long and narrow room and she decorated it with posters of her favorite Hollywood stars. For the last 18 months in the Secret Annex, she had to share the room with Fritz Pfeffer, a jew from Berlin, who she found very annoying. Anne was taken to Aushwitz on August 1944.
